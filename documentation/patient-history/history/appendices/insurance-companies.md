## Usage Notes

1. **Default Selection**: "შიდა" (value: 0) is the default, indicating private/internal payment
2. **Most Common**: National Health Agency (628) for state-covered patients
3. **Special Cases**: "უფასო" (Free) and "დაზღვევის არ მქონე" (Without insurance) for specific patient categories
4. **Value Format**: IDs are numeric, not sequential (indicate database primary keys)

## Business Logic

- When "შიდა" is selected: Patient pays full amount
- When insurance company selected: Insurance coverage % applied to procedures
- Multiple insurance companies can be selected via დაზღვევა I/II/III tabs
- Some institutions (hospitals/clinics) appear in list for direct billing contracts
