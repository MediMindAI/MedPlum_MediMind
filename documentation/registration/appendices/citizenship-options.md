# Citizenship (მოქალაქეობა) Options

## Overview
This appendix documents the complete list of citizenship options available in the patient registration form. This dropdown field allows selection of patient citizenship from 250 countries and territories.

## Field Metadata
- **Field ID**: `mo_moq`
- **Field Label**: მოქალაქეობა (Citizenship)
- **Type**: Single-select dropdown
- **Required**: No
- **Total Options**: 250 (plus 1 empty option)
